Card Collector!

Welcome to the Card Collector game! In this game you will be given a randomly generated number at the start of the game. There are 4 different cards below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a certain amount of points to your total score. You will not know this specific value until you click on a crystal. You will win the game by matching your total score obtained through clicking on crystals to the number randomly generated at the start of the game. Go over the number's value, and you will lose. Each time the game restarts, your crystals will have different values (where would the fun be otherwise???). Good luck!
